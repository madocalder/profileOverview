About the Profile

What is it?

ASTHO’s Profile survey has gathered information on state, territorial, and freely associated state public health agencies (S/THAs) activities, structure, and resources since 2007.

How is it used?

The Profile is the most comprehensive data source about S/THAs in the United States and has many potential applications for public health decision makers, stakeholders, and researchers.

  • Advocacy for public health needs
  • Onboarding new public health leaders
  • Trending public health infrastructure
  • Needs assessments and strategic planning
  • National and cross-jurisdiction comparisons
